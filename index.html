The Makers

In the hands of our artisans.

Behind every piece of Indikarigar jewellery is a master artisan who has spent decades perfecting their craft. These are not just makers — they are keepers of tradition, storytellers through metal and stone.

From the narrow lanes of Jaipur to the ancient workshops of Varanasi, we partner with artisan families who have inherited their skills through generations. Your purchase directly supports their livelihood and preserves these dying art forms.
